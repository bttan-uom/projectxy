About Diabetes@Home & MyGlucose

Who are we and what do we do?

Who is Diabetes@Home?

We are a startup specialising in digital health solutions. Our latest and greatest product, MyGlucose, is a web app that helps people to manage their diabetes in the convenience of their own home, recording data that can be monitored remotely by their clinician. A person with diabetes will need to record data for many years, so MyGlucose by Diabetes@Home is a perfect solution combining great usability, and a positive and motivating user-experience.


You must be logged in to use most of the features of this website, as it contains confidential and personalised information for each user. Unregistered users may use the Home, About Us and About Diabetes pages.

Synchronise your health information

Regular checking and recording of your blood glucose level can help you make healthy lifestyle choices and can alert your clinician to a possible need for a change in how your diabetes is being managed. Effective management of diabetes means aiming for a careful balance between the foods you eat, how active you are and what medication you take.
